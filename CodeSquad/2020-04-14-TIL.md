## Swagger Interceptor 안걸리도록 설정

[참고](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/59568767/excludepattern-is-not-working-for-swagger-url)

```java
registry.addInterceptor(new CustomInterceptor()).addPathPatterns("/**").
            excludePathPatterns(
                    "/v2/api-docs",
                    "/swagger-resources/**",
                    "/swagger-ui.html",
                    "/webjars/**"
            );
```

를 적용하면 된다!
